Thank you for downloading

BlueZone Scripts!

You're almost done!

Now that you have configured your BlueZone Scripts, a *.zip file has been downloaded. Check your downloads folder for it!

You still have some work to do before you get your scripts installed and ready-to-go:

  1. Navigate to wherever your downloads save to. For many folks, it's viewable in a downloads-toolbar below. Otherwise, go wherever you typically find your downloads.
  2. Right click the file, and click "extract all". Windows will ask you where you'd like to extract it. Make sure you extract it to your shared drive folder, exactly as you configured in step 1 on the previous page.
  3. After extracting the file, navigate to your shared drive, and select instructions.html to see the next steps.

What if I need help?

If you run into issues with the next steps, simply contact Veronica Cary from DHS. She can help you out!
